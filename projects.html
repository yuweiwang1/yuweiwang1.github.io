Research

Six projects across two threads — designing safer, more legible interactions between people and vehicles.

In-vehicle informatics

Alert Modality and Response Time in ADAS Interfaces

A mixed-methods user study examining how visual, auditory, and haptic alerts in advanced driver assistance systems affect response time and cognitive workload across varying road complexity levels.

User study · N=32 · Published 2024

Gaze Allocation in Augmented Reality Head-Up Displays

Eye-tracking study examining how drivers distribute visual attention across AR-HUD information layers while navigating complex urban intersections. Compares layer salience strategies and their effect on situation awareness.

Eye-tracking · Driving simulation · In progress

Cognitive Load in Multi-Modal In-Vehicle Systems

Investigating driver cognitive load during concurrent use of navigation guidance, ADAS notifications, and infotainment across three road environments. Proposes an adaptive information filtering model.

AV–pedestrian

Expressive Deceleration Profiles for AV–Pedestrian Communication

Designing and evaluating human-like deceleration motion patterns that communicate yielding intent to pedestrians without relying on external displays. Explores the threshold between legible and uncanny vehicle motion.

Motion design · Pedestrian study · Published 2024

AV–pedestrian

eHMI Icon Comprehension Across Cultural Contexts

Cross-cultural comparison of how pedestrians from five countries interpret external human-machine interface symbols displayed by autonomous vehicles at unsignalised crossings. Identifies universal signals and culturally-specific breakdowns.

Cross-cultural study · N=180 · In progress

Preprint
AV–pedestrian

Trust Calibration in Repeated Urban AV Encounters

A longitudinal scenario-based study exploring how trust in autonomous vehicles develops, stabilises, and breaks down across repeated pedestrian encounters in urban environments. Proposes a three-phase trust calibration model.

Longitudinal study · Mixed methods · Published 2023
